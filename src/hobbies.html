Turning Creativity Into Business: My Journey

I’ve been making friendship bracelets since primary school, and over the years, I've developed more than 10 unique designs that reflect my personal style. In high school, I turned this passion into a small business, selling my creations through my Telegram channel and dedicated Instagram account. This side project has helped me refine my attention to detail, manage an online presence, and engage with a community of customers. It’s a creative outlet that continues to push me to grow and innovate.

Enhancing My Skill Set Through Entrepreneurship

Selling my handmade bracelets has been an incredible learning experience, teaching me valuable skills in branding, marketing, and customer service. Promoting designs, managing sales, and interacting with customers have enhanced my communication and organizational abilities. These experiences demonstrate how I combine creativity with technical skills, which I apply in my professional work as a frontend developer. Balancing this entrepreneurial venture with my studies in software engineering has made me more adaptable and resourceful.
